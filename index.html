Frequently Asked Questions

How does ZEUS help my business get more Google reviews? ▼

ZEUS automates the process of requesting reviews from your customers via SMS and email. After a transaction or service, a personalized message is sent to your customer with a direct link to leave a Google review. This frictionless process significantly increases your review rate compared to manual methods.

Is ZEUS compatible with my existing CRM or job management software? ▼

Yes! ZEUS is designed for seamless integration with many popular CRM and job management applications such as Tradify, ServiceM8, and others. This allows you to trigger review requests directly from your existing workflows without manual data entry.

What if a customer leaves a negative review? ▼

ZEUS focuses on encouraging positive reviews. While we can't prevent negative reviews on Google, our system streamlines the process for satisfied customers, naturally increasing your overall positive review volume. We recommend engaging with all reviews, positive or negative, professionally on your Google My Business profile.

Is there a long-term contract or commitment required? ▼

No, there are no lock-in contracts. ZEUS operates on a simple monthly subscription of $49.99, and you can cancel anytime. We believe in earning your business every month through the value we provide.

How quickly will I see results? ▼

Many of our clients begin to see a significant increase in their Google reviews within the first 2 weeks of using ZEUS, with some reporting a 30% or more uplift. The speed of results can depend on your customer volume and engagement.
